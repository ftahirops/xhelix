Why xhelix is conservative on ML: ML is opaque, hard to debug, easy to poison. Academic research (Holmes 2019) and operational experience both show deterministic LLR scoring with capped per-domain evidence outperforms naive ML for low-FP security decisions. ML adds value as a hint over curated data, not as the primary engine.
