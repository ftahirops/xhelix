Protect-Our-Own Backstop

The immutable list of paths NOTHING can write, quarantine, or delete — not even a correctly-signed BRP. Born from the 2026-05-23 prod scare and the EDR-causing-its-own-incident failure mode.

Why this exists

The EDR capability to quarantine files is real. The cost of being wrong about which files matter is catastrophic. We made the decision once, in code, with version control and code review — rather than at runtime under stress.

Even a correctly-signed BRP is rejected if it grants access here
Profile.Validate() calls IntersectsProtected() on every load. A profile claiming write to /etc/shadow fails validation before signature verification. This is the v2 "curator cannot subvert endpoint" invariant.
